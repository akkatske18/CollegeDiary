INTRODUCTION


The 36th anniversary celebration of the Asian College of Technology (ACT) was a remarkable event filled with excitement and camaraderie. The festivities included dynamic performances from faculty and students alike, showcasing their talents in dance and theater.

The Admin Faculty Night featured a captivating grand finale that left the audience in awe, while the awarding ceremony highlighted the achievements of the competition winners. The venue for Lamparoza, adorned beautifully for College Day, set the perfect backdrop for the celebrations.

Throughout the event, friends and instructors shared memorable moments, captured in selfies and group pictures that reflect the spirit of unity and joy. Overall, the anniversary not only commemorated ACT's legacy but also fostered a sense of community among students, faculty, and staff.
